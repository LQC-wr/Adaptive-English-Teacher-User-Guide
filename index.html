Core features

Built on a complete backend: discovery & recommendation, reading highlights, vocabulary system, auto-generated tests, AI writing/speaking evaluation, chat practice, and stats.

Discover

Interest-based recommendations

Discover new articles by topic, then get hybrid recommendations and similar-article suggestions.

Reading

Highlights + one-tap translation

Vocabulary, collocations, and grammar highlights. Translation uses Gemini first, with reliable fallback.

Vocabulary

Wordbook + standard lists

Save words with definitions/examples/translations. Learn from CET4/CET6/SAT lists + quizzes.

Reading Tests

Auto-generated quizzes

Cloze and True/False questions generated from articles, with instant scoring and history tracking.

Writing Coach

IELTS + general scoring

Strict IELTS criteria scoring + practical writing quality score, plus an improved version and saved history.

Speaking Coach

Whisper STT + IELTS feedback

Speech-to-text transcription and IELTS-style feedback across fluency, vocabulary, and grammar.
